Insightiv Diagnostics Platform


Hybrid teleradiology platform for medical imaging diagnostics and research.


Combining PACS, extensive Imaging Viewer and Artificial Intelligence screening capabilities in a single platform.


Our Solution

Feature-rich medical image viewer, in-workflow reporting and so much more!


A wide range of DICOM modalities can be viewed in a web based cross-platform DICOM viewer that can be accessed on any devices with a web browser and an internet connection.

In-workflow reporting allows medical imaging specialists to create and submit report using a single platform.

Being a cloud-based system, Insightiv Diagnostics Platform allows clinic owners to focus on patient care rather than technical issues. We take care of the platform maintenance, upgrades and deployments.



Suppored Modalities


We support different Modalities including:

Computer Tomography (CT)

Magnetic Resonance (MR)

Computed Radiography (CR)

X-Ray

Ultrasound (US)

Mammography (MG)


The viewer is customized to provide tools based on the image modality being viewed, this allows medical image specialists to access a wide range of tools for better analysis.


Supported Regions


Insightiv Diagnostics Platform is designed to be deployable in any cloud or on-premises environment.

Due to data localization and data residency laws, different nations require citizen's data to be collected, processed, and/or stored inside the country. Insightiv Diagnostics Platform is already deployed in three different continents with four different deployments, Boston - USA, Paris - France, Cape Town - South Africa, Kigali - Rwanda.

Insightiv diagnostics platform can be deployed in any other region upon request. Reach out to info@insightiv.ai for any inquiries.
